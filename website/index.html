CUNY Buzz

About

CUNY Buzz is a Facebook messenger bot that allows you to search for registered campus specific events. This is a website for club officers and other authorized persons to submit their event to our database. To do so please fill out the form on the right. Make sure to include all fields otherwise the form will be considered invalid. To test out the bot yourself click the button bellow or check out our facebook page.
